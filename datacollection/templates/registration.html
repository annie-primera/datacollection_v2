Before you register...

This web application is part of my PhD research. The full information sheet can be found in this link, but the data that will be collected from you will be:

  • Demographic information
  • Time spent using the platform
  • Functions used
  • Feedback uptake
  • Submitted text

All of this data will be stored anonymously.

If you want to withdraw from this study at any moment, for any reason, you can contact me at ana.hibert@ed.ac.uk and I will immediately withdraw you and delete any data collected on you.

By creating this account, you confirm that you are aware that your data will be collected, and that you understand the purposes for which the data will be used, as outlined here.

In order to register for this software, it is important that you fill out all the fields in the registration form. Your information will always remain anonymous.
