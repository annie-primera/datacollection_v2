Frequently asked questions

What is the Readability Score in the summary? Why is my score always so low?

The Readability score lets you know how readable your text would be to an average 13-year old. Postgraduate writing is always going to get a very low score, because your target audience is people with higher degrees and not average 13-year-olds. You do not have to worry about the readability score.

Why does the program hate passive verbs so much?

In most types of writing, passive verbs are frowned upon because it 'hides' who performed an action. In academic writing, passive voice is used sometimes to denote objectivity or to focus on the action performed, but APA still recommends active voice for academic writing. This resource from the University of Toronto in Canada explains the difference between active and passive voice, and this resource from the University of0 Wollonwong in Australia gives some good examples on when passive voice is acceptable.
